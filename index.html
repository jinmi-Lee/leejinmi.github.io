01 — About

Who I am

I'm a Film and Media student at Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU), Seoul, with a deep fascination for the craft behind Korean variety shows.

For me, variety isn't just entertainment — it's a sophisticated system of communication. From the rhythm of a perfectly-timed edit to the graphic punch of a subtitle effect, every element is intentional. I want to decode that system.

My goal is to merge film theory with AI and data visualisation, building tools that reveal what makes these shows resonate so powerfully with their audiences.
